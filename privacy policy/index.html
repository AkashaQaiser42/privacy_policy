Privacy Poliy Page For Patch Customizer

At Patch Customizer, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Patch Customizer, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any sensitive personal information or store data related to your Shopify store's customers, payment methods, or other private store details.

The only data we access or collect is necessary to enable the features of the app, including:

  • Information required to display and apply patches, alphabets, and embroidery to products such as shirts, hoodies, bags, and other items.
  • Basic store information (e.g., shop name, email address) to support app functionality.
  • Product data to apply customizations within your store.

We do not collect any credit card information or personal data from your customers.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is solely used to:

  • Allow users to add custom patches, alphabets, and embroidery to products.
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the app.
  • Provide support and troubleshooting related to app usage.

We do not sell, share, or use your store's data for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Patch Customizer implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third-party services, except as required to support the app's functionality. Any third-party services integrated with Patch Customizer (such as Shopify's APIs) have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the information we collect about your store and to request the deletion of this information if you choose to uninstall the app. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us at akasha@freedommedia.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with the updated date.
